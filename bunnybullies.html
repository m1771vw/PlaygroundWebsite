Bunny Bullies

Bunny Bullies is a multiplayer phone game created for the Computer Game Science Project course at the University of California, Irvine. The team consisted of 5 members and was a project done over 20 weeks.

Technology Stack

C#, Unity

What Was The Game?

The game was a multiplayer Anroid game on the phone. Each player had a screen full of "trash" and needed to swipe the trash into "portals". These "portals" would connect to a certain player, and the special feature of this game is that the trash would go to that player's screen when swiped to. There were 3 rounds and each round lasted _ seconds. At the end of each round, the player would be scored based on how much trash they collected, and how many bonus points they collected.
