Daybreak

Daybreak was a single player platformer Java game created for the University of California, Irvine Video Game Design Club Winter Game Jam of 2012. The team consisted of _ members. This fast paced game won first place in the VGDC Game Jam.

Technology Stack

Java

What Was The Game?

The player is trapped inside a cemetary and has to break the tombstones to free himself from the level. However, the player can only break the tombstones during the day -- once it turned night time, the gravestones are unbreakable and ghosts appear from the broken tombstones and try to hunt you down.
